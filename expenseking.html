HOW TO UPLOAD EXPENSES - in five quick/easy/fun steps :)

STEP 1: Save your expenses in Google DRIVE in CSV format.
STEP 2: Navigate to UPLOAD FROM DRIVE and click on the add new file icon.
STEP 3: Create a configuration for your newly uploaded file. (configurations are used to know things like where are the dates, titles and amounts of your expenses!)
STEP 4: Select your new configuration. The configuration name should now appear as a subtitle of the file.
STEP 5: SYNCHRONIZE! And VOILA all your expenses from your CSV are now uploaded in Expense King!
